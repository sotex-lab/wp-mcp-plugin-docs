What is MCP?

Model Context Protocol (MCP) provides a simple, secure, and reliable way for AI tools to connect with your apps, data, and services. With the MCP Integrate plugin, your WooCommerce store can easily connect to popular AI assistants like ChatGPT and Claude, allowing customers to search for products and interact with your store.

What is ACP?

Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP) is a protocol made by OpenAI with first (currently only) payment provider that utilizes it, enabling customers to complete purchases through ChatGPT. With the MCP Integrate plugin, your store becomes ACP-ready, making it easy for customers to purchase your products via ChatGPT.
