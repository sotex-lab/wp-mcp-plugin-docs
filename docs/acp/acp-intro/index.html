Agentic Commerce Protocol

Design flows for embedded commerce in ChatGPT

Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP) is an open standard created by OpenAI and Stripe that enables smooth interaction between AI agents, buyers, and businesses to complete purchases. It allows merchants to offer instant checkout across web, iOS and Android, while supporting multiple payment methods like cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Link by Stripe and more coming soon.

With ACP, merchants keep full control of their customer relationships—handling orders, payments, and post-purchase experiences directly—while AI agents help guide buyers and keep them informed in real time. The protocol is flexible, secure, and designed to integrate easily with existing commerce systems, making it simple to reach new customers through AI-driven channels.

Learn more on the Official ACP website.
